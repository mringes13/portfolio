Overview

This application is designed to allow users to keep track of their finances manually.

Users will be able to keep track of the money they spend or earn, whether it be from their account, wallet or piggybank.

This app will allow users to categorize their transactions as well as sort by previously defined categoreis.

Additionally, this app allows users to log in and log out to allow for a faster, more secure, transferrable experience.

This app is intended for users who are new to personal finance and are excited to feel in control of their own accounts.

Last updated 12/01
